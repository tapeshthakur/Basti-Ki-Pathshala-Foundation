About Our Foundation

Our Story

Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation was founded by Sunita with a deep passion for social justice and an unwavering belief that education can transform lives. Born from the understanding that every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their circumstances, our foundation has been working tirelessly to bridge the educational gap in underserved communities.

We are registered under the Indian Societies Act of 1860, operating as a grassroots organization committed to sustainable change in slum areas across India.
